PokéBreeder - How Does This Work?

PokéBreeder helps you see what results you would likely get from breeding your Pokémon with others. It does not do the breeding for you or guarantee results; you will have to do that yourself!

Step 1: Select from a list of all the Pokémon to choose the ones you have and would like to breed. After choosing a Type, you can sort the list by name or number using the dropdown menu. You may save up to 12 Pokémon in your Breeding Box. Tap the blue tab at the bottom of the screen to see your saves. Tap the X next to a Pokémon to remove it from your list.

Step 2: Choose a Pokemon from your Breeding Box to be Parent #1. Tapping a Pokemon will bring up a list of their potential partners. Tapping "Breed" next to a second Pokémon will select that Pokémon as Parent #2.

Step 3: Click View Results to see which baby Pokémon you could get!

Repeat as needed and have fun!
